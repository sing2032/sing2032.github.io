About me

I am Jasleen, a student at Algonquin College. I am enrolled in the program of IMD. It is currently my 1st semester, and I am looking forward to exploring this course further in the coming months. Before coming to Canada, I had completed my Senior Secondary education back in India, in the stream of fine arts.

I would like to say that I am an artistic person and enjoy art a lot. I do a bit of digital art when I get the time. I also bought a drawing tablet a year back doing art commissions. It was about $400, and it was a huge accomplishment for me at that time.
